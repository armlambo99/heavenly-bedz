About Heavenly Beds

Our Story

Heavenly Beds is a furniture manufacturer and supplier based in Kempton Park, Gauteng. We specialize in crafting high-quality beds, headboards, couches, and pedestals from scratch, ensuring exceptional craftsmanship and attention to detail in every piece.

What sets us apart is our commitment to manufacturing everything ourselves and selling directly to our customers at factory prices, eliminating middleman costs while maintaining superior quality.

With a focus on both individuals clients and commercial establishments like guesthouses, hotels, and student accommodations, we serve customers across Gauteng and Mpumalanga.
